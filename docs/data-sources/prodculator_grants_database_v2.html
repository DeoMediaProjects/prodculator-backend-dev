Data Source Policy — Legally Safe for Commercial Use: All grant data sourced exclusively from official government portals, national film commissions, publicly funded broadcaster arms (BBC Film, ARTE, RAI Cinema, NHK, ZDF), and publicly documented independent foundation programmes (Sundance Institute, IDA, IDFA, IFFR, Berlinale, Tribeca, Hot Docs). Factual data — amounts, deadlines, eligibility criteria — is not copyrightable under UK and international law. All eligibility descriptions are written in Prodculator's own words. Every entry cites the administering body's official portal URL.

⚠ CRITICAL LEGAL EXPOSURE — Consumer Rights Act 2015 s.49: Grant deadlines and opening dates must be re-verified before every report generation cycle. A paid report surfacing a grant with a passed deadline is a service not performed with reasonable care and skill. Any entry with verified_at older than 6 months must be flagged for re-verification before remaining in active production reports. State-level US grants (Georgia, Louisiana, Illinois, New Mexico) are subject to annual legislative budget approval and can be suspended without notice — verify status each cycle. Nationality restrictions must be surfaced prominently in any user-facing recommendation — failure to do so creates misrepresentation exposure under Misrepresentation Act 1967 s.2.
